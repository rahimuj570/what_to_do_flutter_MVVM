Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Task Reminder App (“What To-Do : Task Reminder”) handles user information. The App is fully offline and open-source. It does not collect, store, or share personal data with third parties.

Information Collection

The App allows users to create, update, and delete to-do tasks. All data is stored locally on the user’s device. No information is transmitted to external servers.

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind users of their tasks. These notifications are generated and managed entirely on the device.

Activity Graph

The App displays an animated activity graph (Lazy, Active, Proactive) based on user task completion. This data is calculated locally and never shared outside the device.

Open Source

The App is open-source. Users and developers can review the source code to verify how data is handled.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with advertising, analytics, or external services. No third-party SDKs are used that collect personal information.

Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

Updates to this Privacy Policy may be made to reflect improvements or compliance requirements. Any changes will be published in the open-source repository and updated on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer via the project’s GitHub repository.
